Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off if desired)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium bowl (or stand mixer with paddle) mix all dough ingredients until the flour is absorbed. No heavy kneading needed; fold the dough a few times to incorporate the last dry bits. Cover and rest.

  2. Fold and let rise

    After 30 minutes perform a few stretch-and-folds from edge to center. Repeat 2â€“3 times during the rise until roughly doubled.

  3. Caramelize the onions

    Cook onions slowly with a little oil and a pinch of salt until deep golden, then splash in the balsamic vinegar to finish.

  4. Stretch, top, and bake

    Transfer dough to an oiled pan, dimple with your fingers, scatter onions, brie, and cranberries, brush with oil, and bake until golden.
